About Keyloggers

This simulation demonstrates how keyloggers work. In a real scenario, keyloggers can be used by cybercriminals to capture sensitive information like passwords and credit card numbers.

How to Protect Yourself:

  • Use antivirus software with real-time protection
  • Keep your operating system and software updated
  • Avoid downloading software from untrusted sources
  • Use two-factor authentication when available
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts

This is a simulation for educational purposes only. No actual keylogging is occurring.
